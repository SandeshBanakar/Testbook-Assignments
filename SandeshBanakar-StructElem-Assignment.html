Kashmiri teacher earns plaudits online with fully-automatic solar car

Bilal Ahmed, a maths teacher from Srinagar, tries to merge innovation with luxury and makes a vehicle that can counter fuel price hikes.
With fuel prices soaring through the roof, people have been looking for alternatives for their mode of transportation. Now, a man from Kashmir is going viral for his creative solution - a car powered by solar energy.

What is the idea behind his invention?

Initially he planned to make a vehicle accessible to people with disabilities. His plans did materialise owing to a lack of funds. However, amid the growing concern about fuel price hikes, he evolved his plans to turn it into a car that runs on solar power.
